Welcome to Gurez

A Sanctuary on the
Kishanganga Riverbanks

Dardic Resort Gurez is a premium boutique retreat nestled along the crystal-clear banks of the legendary Kishanganga River. Here, the Himalayas rise in silent majesty around you, and every sunrise paints the sky gold over the iconic Habba Khatoon Peak.

Situated in the pristine Gurez Valley — once an ancient stop on the historic Silk Route — our resort offers an unparalleled blend of natural grandeur and warm Kashmiri hospitality.

Nearby Attractions

Gurez Valley is a treasure trove of natural wonders, ancient history, and adventure. Explore from our doorstep.

Habba Khatoon Peak

The iconic peak named after Kashmir's beloved poetess — visible from the resort.

Crystal-clear Himalayan river flowing right beside the resort — perfect for reflection.

Trekking Routes
