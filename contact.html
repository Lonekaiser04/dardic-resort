How to Reach Gurez

By Road: Gurez is accessible via the Razdan Pass from Bandipora (~85 km). The scenic road is open from May to November.

By Air: Nearest airport is Sheikh ul-Alam International Airport, Srinagar (~130 km from Gurez).

Note: Inner Line Permit (ILP) may be required for non-J&K residents. Contact us for the latest travel advisories.

Everything you need to know before planning your stay at Dardic Resort Gurez.

Gurez Valley is best visited from May to October. The valley is most accessible and at its lush green best from June to September. The road via Razdan Pass typically opens in May and closes in November due to heavy snowfall.
Non-J&K residents may require an Inner Line Permit (ILP) to visit Gurez Valley, as it is a border area. The permit is usually obtained from the Deputy Commissioner's office in Bandipora. Please check current requirements and contact us for the latest information before your visit.
