How to Reach Gurez

By Road: Accessible via the Razdan Pass from Bandipora (~85 km). Road is open from May to November.

By Air: Nearest airport is Sheikh ul-Alam International, Srinagar (~130 km from Gurez).

Note: Inner Line Permit (ILP) may be required for non-J&K residents. Contact us for latest travel info.

Everything you need to know before planning your stay at Dardic Resort Gurez.

Gurez Valley is best visited from May to October. The valley is most accessible and lush from June to September. The road via Razdan Pass typically opens in May and closes in November due to heavy snowfall.
Non-J&K residents may require an Inner Line Permit (ILP) to visit Gurez Valley, as it is a border area. The permit is usually obtained from the Deputy Commissioner's office in Bandipora. Contact us for the latest information before your visit.
